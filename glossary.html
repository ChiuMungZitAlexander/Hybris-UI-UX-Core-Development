a

Achieve Magazine
The Quixtar magazine for Independent Business Owners, about IBOs and their achievements.
Achievers Invitational
A Business Conference that qualified Platinums may be invited to attend as part of the Quixt
Automatic Shipments
(Auto Ship) Platinums may instruct Quixtar to automatically ship them product to make up for a shortage of volume (up to a specific percent) qualifications. If Quixtar determines the IBO has missed the specified qualification, as well as other applicable incentive program qualifications, and the shortage falls within the specified percentage margin, an order is prepared from a list of predetermined product, shipped and billed by credit card.
Awards and Bonuses
To recognize and reward IBO achievements, Quixtar has an extensive award and bonus system. Pins and plaques are awarded in honor of business-building achievements, and recognition is given in the Achieve® Magazine. To be eligible for these awards, an IBO must be in compliance with all the provisions of the Code of Ethics and the Rules of Conduct for Quixtar IBOs.
Award Volume
Includes an IBO's Personal Volume and all downline Associate IBO volume; it does not include the volume from 25% IBOs. Award Volume is used to determine Silver and Platinum qualification months. Negative volume is included/applied.

b

Bonus Information Summary (BIS)
Available online to all IBOs who qualify for a bonus and are paid by Quixtar. The BIS lists all IBOs within a group, in line-of-sponsorship order. The Personal PV/BV, Group PV/BV, Personal A/R, and Group A/R amounts are displayed for each Quixtar-powered business owner, based on orders and other transactions through Quixtar. The applicable bonus statements are included.
Bracket
IBOs are paid a Performance Bonus based on their monthly Group PV bracket.
BSMAA
Business Support Materials Arbitration Agreement. Introduced at registration, BSMAA applies the same arbitration procedures used in disputes related to business and applies to any disputes involving separate Business Support Materials-related issues. The BSMAA SA 2033 [PDF] is available online.
Builder
An IBO that retails product and sponsors others in the business.
Bundles
Special groupings of related products (for example, laundry care, skin care, nutritional) that Quixtar packages occasionally for promotional purposes.
